Intercontinetal Wellbeing Services (IWS)

Intercontinental Well-Being Services Limited, (IWS) is a company that focuses on providing public education, research, and advocacy on mental health to the community, primarily to the corporate world, education institutes, and peer groups to enable healthy, meaningful life and economic agility in Tanzania and beyond.
